# Daily Standup

## Purpose

The Daily Standup is a short, daily alignment meeting for the Scrum team. It makes progress visible, identifies impediments early, and coordinates collaboration during the sprint.

## Guidelines

- **Duration:** max. 15 minutes
- **Frequency:** daily, same time each day
- **Participants:** Development team, Scrum Master (Product Owner optional)
- **Format:** Each team member answers three guiding questions
